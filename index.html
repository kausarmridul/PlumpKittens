Roadmap

As our fluffy family grows we’ll be finding cute and creative ways to show our appreciation. So take a look below at what we have in store for you krazy Kittens.

Through the Catflap

Our journey will begin by engaging with you and exploring our paw-sibilities! We’ll start with a detailed marketing campaign and by driving this project to purr-fection with the community onboard.

Once we’re fur-miliar we’ll look to host a range of giveaways, rewarding our keenest Kittens.

The Lucky Litter

At 50% minted we’ll be hosting a raffle, where 10 of our lucky plumpy pals will be in with the chance of winning $100 worth of ETH.

Only holders that have minted will be allowed to enter the raffle, so make sure you get those paws on the prize.

Adoption Time

We might have come far, but not far enough. 10 of our cutest Kittens will be airdropped to 10 fur-tunate NFT holders.

Will you give them a loving home?

The Purrfect Project

We did it! Each of our Plumpy Kittens has a brand new home, and we can initiate our roadmap utility to ensure long term pleasure for our plumpy pals

We’ll start with a 5000 $ donation to a cat rescue shelter, decided upon by our community.

Oh, and we’ll be airdropping free Plump Kittens for those that are active on the secondary market.
